Prototype · Vexa + external transcription

Send a bot into a meeting and pull the transcript back into your workspace.

Paste a Google Meet, Zoom, or Teams link. The prototype will parse the URL, send a bot through Vexa, and poll the transcript endpoint so you can see what was captured.

How this prototype works

  • Capture: your link is parsed into the Vexa meeting format.
  • Bot join: Vexa starts a bot and joins the meeting as a participant.
  • Transcription: Vexa Lite streams audio into an OpenAI-compatible transcription endpoint.
  • Next step: this transcript feed can later be pushed into Layers Pages and Tasks.
